Q3 Write a python program to find the square of the digit of a number

answer 3

x = input("enter your number  ")
sq=0
s=len(x)
lis=[]
for i in range(1,s+1):
    y=x[i-1:i]
    r=int(y)
    sq=sq+r*r
    lis=lis+[r*r]
print("this is your number", x,"this is your squre list", lis,"this is squre sum  ",sq)
